DHA LICENSED

Registered General Nurse

Dubai, UAE Full-time Immediate Start

WHAT YOU'LL DO

  • Provide high-quality clinical nursing care to patients in their homes.
  • Monitor patient vital signs and administer medications as prescribed.
  • Coordinate with doctors and families to ensure comprehensive care plans.
  • Wound dressing, IV infusions, and post-surgical recovery monitoring.
  • Educate family members on patient care and health management.

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

  • Valid DHA (Dubai Health Authority) Nursing License.
  • Proven experience as a Registered Nurse in a hospital or home care setting.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Compassionate approach and dedication to patient well-being.
  • Ability to work autonomously and handle emergency situations.
SPECIALIZED

Pediatric Physiotherapist

Dubai, UAE Full-time 2+ Years Exp.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

  • Conduct assessments of children with physical or developmental challenges.
  • Develop and implement pediatric physiotherapy treatment plans.
  • Work with families and multidisciplinary teams to support child development.
  • Utilize therapeutic exercises and specialized equipment.
  • Track progress and adjust treatment strategies as needed.

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

  • Degree in Physiotherapy from a recognized institution.
  • Minimum 2 years experience in pediatric physiotherapy.
  • DHA License as a Physiotherapist.
  • Patient and encouraging attitude towards children and families.
  • Knowledge of developmental milestones and specialized techniques.
CAREGIVING

Home Care Assistant

Dubai, UAE Part-time / Full-time Compassionate Care

WHAT YOU'LL DO

  • Assist patients with daily living activities (bathing, dressing, grooming).
  • Provide companionship and emotional support to patients.
  • Prepare healthy meals based on dietary requirements.
  • Help with light housekeeping and errands as needed.
  • Monitor patient well-being and report changes to the medical team.

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

  • Previous experience in caregiving or a related healthcare role.
  • Deep compassion and patience for working with elderly or disabled patients.
  • Ability to perform physical tasks related to patient assistance.
  • Good communication skills and reliability.
  • Current First Aid/CPR certification is a plus.
