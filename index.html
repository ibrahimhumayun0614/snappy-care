ABOUT SNAPPY CARE

Dedicated to exemplary care within our community.

Established in 2020, Snappy Care is the entity of Saif Belhasa Holding. Snappy Care emerged from a profound commitment to delivering exemplary care to our community. Recognizing the dual of supporting the younger and older generations, we founded our company to address these critical needs comprehensively.

Adhering strictly to the guidelines set forth by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the Dubai Health Authority (DHA), we ensure the delivery of superior quality cares.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our home care services and how we can support your health journey.

What makes Snappy Care the best home healthcare service provider?

Snappy Care is recognized as one of the best home healthcare service providers in Dubai offering personalized and professional medical services directly to your home. Our team of DHA-licensed professionals ensures compassionate care, making us a trusted choice for families seeking quality home health services.

What types of home healthcare services do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive range of home healthcare services in Dubai, including elderly care, pregnancy care, babycare, nursing services, physiotherapy care, doctor on call, lab tests, IV therapy, wound care, and specialized support for children with autism.

Who can benefit from home health care services?

Home health care services from Snappy Care benefit a wide range of individuals, especially those needing assistance at home due to medical or personal reasons. Elderly individuals requiring daily support, patients recovering from surgeries, and those with chronic conditions can all benefit from home care in Dubai. New mothers, babies, and individuals needing physiotherapy or specialized medical treatments like IV therapy also find these services helpful.

How quickly can I receive services from Snappy Care?

Our team is available 24/7, and we strive to provide prompt services, often within an hour of booking, ensuring timely medical attention when you need it most.

What are the payment channels available?

You can make the payment using a card, online transfer, or cheque—whichever is most convenient for you.

What is the refund policy of Snappycare?

We will refund if service was not satisfactory and if its within 24 hrs.

Are services covered by insurance in the UAE?

Coverage depends on your specific insurance plan. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to confirm if our services are included in your policy.

Can I customize the services to fit my needs?

Absolutely. We tailor our services to meet individual health requirements, ensuring personalized care plans that align with each client’s specific needs.

Why choose Snappy Care over other providers?

Snappy Care stands out for its commitment to delivering high-quality, patient-centered care. Our experienced professionals, comprehensive service offerings, and dedication to client satisfaction position us as a leading choice for home healthcare services in the UAE.

Do you offer pediatric home healthcare services?

Yes, we provide specialized pediatric care, including babysitting services, developmental support, and medical care tailored to children’s unique needs.
